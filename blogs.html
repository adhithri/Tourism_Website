Gelmerbahn,Bern, Switzerland


If you are looking for a real adventure, the Gelmerbhan is for you! With a gradient of 106 percent. It is undoubtedly one the steepest passenger funicular railways in Europe. I t takes visitors on scenic 12 minutes ride with a steep climb and dreamy mountainscape backdrops.
